Usage

Protect the quiet.

Do

  • Keep the lines thin and the space around the mark generous.
  • Hold the weight contrast: heavier line leads, lighter line follows.
  • Use brass only as hairline and axis, never as a fill.
  • Default to paper and indigo grounds; let layouts breathe.

Don't

  • Thicken the strokes into a bold or filled mark.
  • Add the play, record, or rung elements back in.
  • Introduce a third colour, gradients, or shadows.
  • Crowd the mark or pair it with any font but Spectral and Inter.
